About This Project

This website was created to provide youth in Serutle and Manamela villages with essential knowledge and resources to improve their lives. We focus on practical, actionable information that can help with education, skills development, and personal growth.

Our goal is to make useful information easily accessible to young people who may not have reliable access to career guidance or educational resources.
